Home Workout Routine for Fat Loss & Abs

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Jumping Jacks – 1 minute
  • High Knees – 1 minute
  • Arm Circles – 30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward
  • Leg Swings – 30 seconds each leg

Main Workout (Repeat 3-4 Times)

  • Burpees – 10-15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers – 30 reps (15 per leg)
  • Plank to Push-Up – 10-12 reps
  • Bicycle Crunches – 20 reps (10 per side)
  • Leg Raises – 15-20 reps
  • Flutter Kicks – 20-30 seconds
  • Bodyweight Squats – 15-20 reps
  • Plank Hold – 30-60 seconds

Cool Down (5-10 minutes)

  • Cobra Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Child's Pose – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Hip Flexor Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds each side

Stay consistent and focus on form! Repeat this workout 4-5 times a week for best results.
